<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Objective:</strong></span> Gain experience with UX design and menus in Android</p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Prep: </strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Details Part 1:</strong></span></p>
<p>Evaluate the menu system of two android apps. Try to avoid games and apps with custom interfaces. Select two apps and explore their use of menus including the action bar menu, the floating action button, and the navigation drawer. For each app, answer the following:</p>
<ol>
<li>Does the app use an action bar menu? What menu items are displayed on the action bar and what menu items appear in the pull down menu <img src="https://cdn4.iconfinder.com/data/icons/aami-web-internet/64/aami12-51-512.png" width="32" height="32" /></li>
<li>Does the app use a floating action button? If so, what does it do?</li>
<li>Does the app use a navigation drawer that appears on the right side when you press the menu icon <img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/Fw96Z.png" width="32" height="32" /></li>
<li>Does the screen switch when you swipe right or left?</li>
</ol>

<p>Make the following modifications to the GitHub project you downloaded in the Prep.</p>
